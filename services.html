Our Services

Warehousing and Distribution

We take care of all the processes involved in the storage and handling of your goods. We also perform product mixing, cross-docking, order fulfillment, and packaging.

Cargo Insurance

The movement of goods across the world comes with certain risks. We insure goods against potential losses which may occur during air, sea or land transportation.

Global Shipping

Ours is a worldwide freight forwarding operation providing a wide range of services to support your shipping needs. We make sure that the world is at your palm.

Ocean Freighting

Over 90% of all the world's trade is carried by sea. We will put your business on the map by allowing you to import or export at reasonable rates.

Full Container Loads (FCL)

FCL is the standard form of shipping for a large cargo. It's a shipment type where a cargo occupies the full size of a container. Charged on the basis of a full container.

Refrigerated Containers (Reefers)

Refrigerated containers are big, mobile fridges used to transport and store perishable goods at the perfect temperature, in any environment.

Break Bulk Cargo

Breakbulk ocean shipping is a common method used to successfully transport cargo or goods that cannot fit in standard-size shipping containers or cargo bins.

Air Freight

We transfer and ship goods via air carriers, which may be charter or commercial. Such shipments travel out of commercial and passenger aviation gateways to the world.

Less than Container Loads (LCL)

LCL describes container loads filled by multiple orders. When shipping a small volume of stock by container ship, it's more economical to combine your orders.

Freight Tracking

Freight tracking is a critical component of any logistics process. We offer a personalized endpoint for you to track your shipments in real time 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Less than Truckload (LTL)

Our parcel carriers also handle small packages and freight that can be broken down into units less than approximately 150 pounds.

Refrigerated Cargo

We transport perishable cargo, which require temperature-controlled handling, such as fruits, meat, vegetables, dairy products, and similar items.

Rail Freight Cargo

Trains are not only the safest but also the cheapest way to ship your cargo across land. We will handle containerization, packing, transport, and unpacking of your rail freight.

Vehicle Shipping

Want to import of export a car from or to overseas? We handle the logistical nightmare that vehicle shipping can be whether it is a single car or several containers.

Business Shipping

We recognize the fact that businesses require special treatment from individual shippers. We charge prices with discounts reasonable to your profit margins.
